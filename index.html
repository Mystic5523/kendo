Mission Statement

Our mission is to improve the physical health and performance of Kendoka. Through customized fitness programs that compliment a kendoka’s current kendo training as well as coaching and consultation, we specifically target their fitness goals and cater to their individual situation. With a focus on muscular performance and injury prevention, FTFK works to ensure our clients are at their best and may pursue their goals both in kendo and in life.


Our Services

There are many barriers everyone faces in fitness. From time management, physical conditioning, lifestyle changes and more. Finding a trainer who understands your unique situation and the specific demands kendo places on you doesn’t have to be one of them. At Fitness Training For Kendo LLC we are dedicated to helping you through each challenge while providing our services to you in a convenient and affordable way that fits into your schedule and lifestyle. Every plan includes:

  • Online interview.
  • Personalised fitness programs built around client goals, needs, limitations and lifestyle.
  • Regular online consultation to assist and guide clients through their specific fitness program.
  • Time management, health habits, motivation and adherence coaching and relapse prevention strategies.

Our Process

  • Step 1: Getting started.
  • Click the “Get Started” link to download our short health questionnaire and Email it back to us. We will follow up with a Health History Questionnaire if appropriate and may request information from your most recent physical exam. If you don’t have this information we may ask you to obtain a physical exam. After we’ve processed your information we will contact you to schedule an online interview.

  • Step 2: The interview!
  • During the interview we will get to know you and discuss your health situation, needs and goals, as well as the services we provide. Once we’ve determined if FTFK is right for you. We’ll ask you to submit a payment for your plan. Once processed we will begin to develop your personalised training program together in order to ensure we develop the best possible program for your individual needs. You will be sent an email with your exercise program which includes detailed instructions for each exercise.

  • Step 3: Exercise and performance evaluation.
  • You will be asked to make regular recordings of your exercises and submit them to FTFK for us to evaluate in order for us to make any needed changes to your form or program. You will also be asked to submit a regular exercise evaluation questionnaire to us that gives us a brief overview of how the program feels for you.

  • Step 4: Follow ups.
  • We will schedule regular follow up interviews to go over thies corrections and insure you are getting the most out of your program. As well as addressing any other health and fitness needs you require such as time management, healthy eating habits, and relapse prevention strategies.
